<p><i>Claw</i> is a read-only client for the <a href='https://lobste.rs/' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>lobste.rs</a> link aggregator with support for saving posts, functioning as a reading list.</p><p>I built this app to both make it easier to find and remember interesting things from lobste.rs, as well as cut my teeth on Jetpack Compose and build something that I would actually use.</p>
